Treatment of Malnutrition for Infants and Pregnant Women in Gaza Strip

The ongoing aggression on the people of Gaza Strip for more than 11 months has resulted in 50,000 children in northern Gaza suffering from malnutrition. During the war period, at least 60,000 pregnant women suffered from malnutrition, and about 45 people, including children, died due to the same suffering. In southern Gaza, nearly 4,000 children are suffering from symptoms of severe acute malnutrition that can lead to death, and thousands more lack nutritional supplements, therapeutic foods, and appropriate treatments for malnutrition cases ranging from normal to acute and severe.
